About Lake Zone Academic Committee

Lake Zone Academics Committee

The Lake Zone Academic Committee is a regional educational body responsible for coordinating and supervising assessment activities for secondary schools students across the six Lake Zone regions of Tanzania: Geita, Kagera, Mara, Mwanza, Shinyanga, and Simiyu.

It plays a crucial role in preparing students for the official NECTA examinations by providing regional examinations before the final NECTA exams. These assessments measure academic readiness, support teachers in tracking progress, and help students identify areas needing improvement.

Purpose

The Committee ensures standardized, fair, and high-quality assessments across the region while improving performance through reliable exam administration, moderation, and timely release of results.

Background

The Academics Committee was established to unify and organize the exam processes for all Lake Zone regions. With increasing numbers of schools and students, the Committee ensures secure exam distribution, quality control, and consistent evaluation structures aligned with NECTA expectations.

Our Commitment

  • Provide reliable and high-quality regional exams
  • Ensure fairness and transparency in assessments
  • Release results on time to support NECTA readiness
  • Work closely with schools to strengthen academic performance
  • Promote improved standards of education in all six regions
