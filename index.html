Lake Zone Mock Exam Registration Periods

Examination Guidelines

NECTA provide techniques for preparing assessment tools and procedures that accommodate all types of learners, regardless of their ability and needs

Lake Zone Mock Examinations

Lake zone mock examinations occurs from (July - August)-Form Four Lake Zone Mock Examination and from (November - December)-Form Six Lake Zone Mock Examination each year.
