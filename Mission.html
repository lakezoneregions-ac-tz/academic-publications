Our Vision

A visionary committee committed to advancing academic excellence and self-efficacy, setting the national standard for educational leadership.

Our Mission

To provide reliable and high-quality educational assessments to all secondary schools within the six lake zone regions -Geita, Kagera, Mara, Mwanza, Shinyanga and Simiyu in order to promote continuous improvement in academic performance

Our Motto

“Quality assessments build great scholars, and excellent examinations shape our academic success.”
